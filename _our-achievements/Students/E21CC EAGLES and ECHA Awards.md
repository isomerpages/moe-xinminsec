<p><strong><u>E21CC EAGLES Award</u></strong>
</p>
<p>The Edusave Awards for Achievement, Good Leadership &amp; Service (EAGLES)
recognise students who demonstrate leadership qualities, excellence in
non-academic activities, and service to community &amp; schools.</p>
<p>With effect from 2024, EAGLES has been enhanced to recognise students
with consistent and exemplary demonstrate Emerging 21 Century Competencies
(E21CC). These competencies are essential skills that students will need
to thrive in and beyond school. In addition to the Achievement, Leadership
&amp; Service domains, there is a new E21CC domain included to the award.</p>
<p>Nominations for the E21CC domain of the EAGLES Award may be submitted
via this form: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/xms21ccnomination26" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/xms21ccnomination26</a>
</p>
