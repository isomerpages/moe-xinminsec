<h2>Our Learning Experience Framework</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Learning Experience Framework/XMS_Learning_Experience_Framework_2026___Model.jpg">
</div>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>At the heart of the framework is <strong>our school vision, guiding all that we work towards</strong> in
Xinmin – <em>Leading Hearts, Leading Minds</em>. The R<sup>3</sup> represents
our school values of <em>Respect, Responsibility, and Resilience</em>, which
form the core of one’s character.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The second ring describes <strong>our approach in designing learning experiences</strong> for
our students: considering:&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><em>Goals &amp; Content</em>, what students should understand and be able
to do;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><em>Pedagogy</em>, how teachers bring about learning; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><em>Assessment</em>, how students gather information about their progress
and act on feedback to move learning forward.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The third ring describes <strong>the five components of our total curriculum</strong>,
i.e. all the learning experiences Xinmin offers to provide a well-rounded
and future-ready education for our students:</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><em>Level Themes &amp; Cohort Curriculum</em>, providing shared and progressive
learning experiences for each cohort;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><em>Academic Subjects</em>, developing knowledge, skills, and dispositions
across various disciplines;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><em>Co-Curricular Activities</em>, developing joy of learning, student
agency, character, and emerging 21<sup>st</sup> Century Competencies in
the CCA context;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><em>Talent Development</em>, pulling together all the learning experiences
and opportunities through a coherent, school-wide approach; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p> <em>Signature Programmes</em>, leveraging Xinmin’s resources as a forward-looking
school with MOE autonomous status.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The fourth ring describes <strong>the Desired Xinmin Outcomes (DXOs), articulating the qualities developed in Xinmin graduates</strong>: <em>curious learners</em>, <em>compassionate contributors</em>,
and <em>confident leaders</em>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
