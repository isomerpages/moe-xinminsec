<p>School users may connect to SWN@SSOE WIFI to access the internet for teaching and learning purposes.</p><p><strong>To connect to SWN@SSOE</strong>&nbsp;WIFI:</p><p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;On your device, under “<strong>Settings</strong>”, select "<strong>Connections</strong>", enable your "<strong>WIFI</strong>".<br>2.&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;Select “<strong>SWN@SSOE</strong>”.<br><em>(If you are unable to find “SWN@SSOE”, please click&nbsp;<a href="https://xinminsec-moe-edu-sg-admin.cwp.sg/resources/students/helpdesk/swn-at-ssoe-login-guide/swn2-4g-at-ssoe-login-guide" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>)</em></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width:70%;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/swn1.jpeg"></div><p>3.&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;Upon connected to “SWN@SSOE” WIFI, you will be redirected to&nbsp;<strong>SWN@SSOE login page</strong>.<br>-&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;If you are not directed to the login page, go to “<strong>http://portal.swn.moe.edu.sg</strong>”</p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width:70%;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/swn3.jpeg"></div><p>-&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;Check the “<strong>I accept the terms and disclaimer agreement</strong>”.<br>-&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;Login with your&nbsp;<strong>IAMS credentials</strong>&nbsp;to continue</p><p><em>If you have forgotten your IAMS password, please fill up the online form&nbsp;<a href="https://form.gov.sg/#!/5bd66ae43302d5000fd9a2c4" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>. Alternatively, you may wish to fill up the&nbsp;</em><strong><em>hardcopy password reset</em></strong><em>&nbsp;form available outside staff room 2 level 2.</em></p><p>4.&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;If you are connecting to “<strong>SWN@SSOE</strong>” for the first time, please select “<strong>Click Here</strong>” to download and install “MOE-MSS” certificate. (You only need to install once).</p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width:70%;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/swn4.jpeg"></div><p>5.&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;You should see this page upon successfully login to "<strong>SWN@SSOE</strong>" WIFI.</p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width:70%;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/swn5.jpeg"></div><p>6.&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;Remember to log out after you have finished using “<strong>SWN@SSOE</strong>” WIFI.&nbsp;<br>To logout off "<strong>SWN@SSOE</strong>”, simply:<br>-&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;Go to "<strong>http://portal.swn.moe.edu.sg</strong>",<br>-&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;On the top of the screen, click on your “<strong>NRIC</strong>” and select “<strong>Sign Out</strong>”.</p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width:70%;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/swn6.jpeg"></div><p></p>
