<h2>Our Vision 愿景</h2>
<p>Leading Hearts, Leading Minds
<br>
<br>领袖之德，领袖之智</p>
<h2>Our Mission 使命</h2>
<p>To develop every Xinmin student to be a curious learner, a compassionate
contributor and a confident leader.
<br>
<br>培养每一位新民学子成为孜孜不倦的学习者、仁心仁德的贡献者、信心十足的领袖者。</p>
